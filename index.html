The perfect PB&J

Description:

The peanut Butter jelly sandwich is the best sandwich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right, any peanut butter and jam will work.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of all natural peanut butter

Directions:

  1. Lay out both pieces of bread
  2. apply peanut butter and Jelly to bread
  3. Combine both breads
